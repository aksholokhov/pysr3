Welcome to PySR3 documentation!
===================================

SR3 is a relaxation method designed for accurate feature selection. It
currently supports:

-  Linear Models (L0, LASSO, A-LASSO, CAD, SCAD)
-  Linear Mixed-Effect Models (L0, LASSO, A-LASSO, CAD, SCAD)
